We've got what you need

You're probably asking yourself what we have, right? Well, we have the motive and drive to learn new things, to build cool stuff and to help solve problems with technology. We'll help you network and launch your career in the tech field. The purpose of the club is to foster the growing culture of Python learning and development within the community of Harare Institute of Technology and to encourage members to seek out opportunities to expand their knowledge, develop and improve their skills in the programming language. Being deliberate in a community helps us all bring each other up from all aspects, and somehow we hope everyone reaches their milestones from participation.
